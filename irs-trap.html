This isn't a political statement. It isn't partisan outrage. This is a record of what happened to one small business owner in America. This is how systemic problems, left unaddressed, are destroying small businesses across the country.

— Written from experience. Presented as evidence.
The testimony

Oh, the dreams we once had,

How they soared, how they flew!

A business to grow, a life built anew.

But beware of the beast called the IRS pen,

It takes down us dreamers again and again.

It started with payroll, a tax gone awry,

An error unnoticed, left high in the sky.

Though we had an agreement, the penalties grew.

In ways and in places that I never knew!

Oh, levies, and letters, and balances steep,

They pounced on my bank, leaving nothing to keep.

It shocked me. It scared me. I felt so ashamed.

I knew I was trapped in the IRS game.

No matter my efforts, no matter my plea,

It fell on deaf ears as they "collected" from me.

With each passing day and each passing night,

I was sick with the weight of this ongoing fight,

I cried and I worried, I yelled in despair,

The burden so heavy; NO JUSTICE. NOT FAIR.

No help came to guide me, no solace, no aid,

Just silence from all while the bills went unpaid.

For a system so broken, it's wrong, it's not right,

To leave us to suffer, with no end in sight.

Now I wonder, I question, I shout to the skies,

Where is the trust, the truth, and the why?

Congress, dear Congress, the power is yours,

To fix what is broken, to open new doors.

For small business owners, we ask, we implore,

Make fairness the rule, and abuse be no more.

System Failure.

What WILLFUL looks like.

What began in the 1950's as a tool to punish corporate fraud has become a modern-day mechanism that punishes desperate small business owners for surviving as long as they could.

What the record shows
01
One mistake. Endless penalties.

Miss a payroll tax deposit — even once, even by accident — and the IRS stacks penalties on top of penalties. Then charges interest on all of it. The original mistake grows into a debt you can never catch up to.

02
Paying doesn't make it stop.

Even if you set up a payment plan, the penalties and interest keep building while you pay. You can do everything right — and still fall further behind.

03
They can empty your bank account. Today.

The IRS can seize everything in your business or personal bank account with little warning. No court order. No negotiation. One levy and the money you needed to make payroll, pay rent, or keep the lights on — is gone.

04
There is help. You just can't reach it.

The Taxpayer Advocate Service exists to protect people in exactly this situation. But it is underfunded, overwhelmed, and out of reach for most small business owners fighting alone.

05
Big corporations don't face this alone. You do.

Large businesses have tax attorneys and accountants on staff. Small business owners have themselves. The system is the same — but the fight is not.
